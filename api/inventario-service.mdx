## Responsibilities

- Product catalog management
- Raw material tracking
- Recipe management
- Production batch tracking (FIFO)
- Inventory calculations
- Waste management (mermas)
- Purchase order tracking
- Production planning

## Main Entities

### Producto
Finished products:
- Product details (name, type, price)
- Stock levels
- Production batches

### MateriaPrima
Raw materials:
- Material details
- Current stock
- Unit of measurement
- Purchase history

### Receta
Product recipes:
- Ingredient requirements
- Quantities needed
- Recipe type (simple/master)

### LoteProducto
Production batches:
- Batch tracking
- FIFO inventory
- Cost calculations
- Expiration dates

### LoteMateriaPrima
Material batches:
- Material batch tracking
- FIFO for materials
- Purchase tracking

## Controllers

The service has 10+ controllers:

1. **ProductoController**: Product CRUD
2. **LoteProductoController**: Production batch management
3. **MateriaPrimaController**: Raw material management
4. **RecetaController**: Recipe management
5. **RecetaMaestraController**: Master recipes
6. **CompraMateriaPrimaController**: Purchase orders
7. **InsumoProduccionController**: Production inputs
8. **MermaProductoController**: Waste tracking
9. **LoteController**: Batch operations
10. **ReporteInventarioController**: Inventory reports

For detailed endpoint documentation, see [Inventario Endpoints](/api/endpoints/inventario).

## Database Schema

Complex schema with 10+ tables:

**productos:**
- id, nombre, tipo, precio_venta

**materias_primas:**
- id, nombre, unidad_medida, stock_actual

**recetas:**
- id, producto_id, tipo_receta

**lotes_producto:**
- id, producto_id, cantidad, costo_produccion, fecha_produccion, fecha_vencimiento

**lotes_materia_prima:**
- id, materia_prima_id, cantidad, fecha_compra, fecha_vencimiento

**insumos_produccion:**
- Links materials to production batches

**merma_producto:**
- Waste tracking records

## Dependencies

Key dependencies:
- Spring Boot Starter Web
- Spring Boot Starter Data JPA
- Spring Cloud Eureka Client
- PostgreSQL Driver
- MapStruct 1.6.3
- Lombok

## Integration

Integrates with:
- **Eureka Server**: Service registration
- **API Gateway**: Routes from `/api/inventario/**`
- **PostgreSQL**: Data persistence

## Key Features

### FIFO Inventory

Implements First-In-First-Out for:
- Product batches
- Material batches
- Automatic stock deduction from oldest batches

### Cost Calculation

Automatic cost calculation based on:
- Material costs from purchases
- Recipe requirements
- Production quantities

### Stock Alerts

Monitors:
- Low stock levels
- Expiring batches
- Critical materials

For more details, see [Inventory Management](/features/inventory-management).
