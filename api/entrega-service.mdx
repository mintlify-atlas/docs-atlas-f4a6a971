## Responsibilities

- Delivery route management
- Driver assignment
- Delivery recording
- Route optimization with OR-Tools
- Delivery scheduling
- Session management
- Delivery history tracking
- Sales and delivery reporting

## Main Entities

### Ruta
Delivery routes:
- Route details (name, description)
- Assigned drivers
- Active/inactive status
- Associated clients

### RegistroEntrega
Delivery records:
- Delivery details
- Movement type (VENTA, DEVOLUCION, INTERCAMBIO)
- Product quantities
- Payment amounts
- Timestamps

### ProgramacionEntrega
Scheduled deliveries:
- Future delivery planning
- Product requirements
- Client assignments

### SesionReparto
Delivery sessions:
- Session tracking
- Route-driver-date association
- Session status
- Financial totals

### Cliente (referenced)
Client assignments to routes

## Controllers

### EntregaController
Manages deliveries:
- Active route management
- Delivery recording
- Delivery history
- Session management

### RutaController
Manages routes:
- Route CRUD operations
- Driver assignments
- Client associations
- Route optimization

For detailed endpoint documentation, see [Entrega Endpoints](/api/endpoints/entregas).

## Database Schema

**rutas:**
- id, nombre, descripcion, activa

**registros_entrega:**
- id, sesion_reparto_id, cliente_id, tipo_movimiento, fecha_entrega, monto_total

**programacion_entrega:**
- id, ruta_id, cliente_id, producto_id, cantidad, fecha_programada

**sesiones_reparto:**
- id, ruta_id, usuario_id, fecha, estado

**rutas_clientes:**
- ruta_id, cliente_id (many-to-many)

**rutas_usuarios:**
- ruta_id, usuario_id (many-to-many)

## Key Features

### Route Optimization

Integrates with OR-Tools to:
- Calculate optimal delivery sequences
- Minimize travel distance
- Consider client locations
- Reduce delivery time by 20-40%

### Delivery Types

Supports multiple movement types:
- **VENTA**: Regular sale delivery
- **DEVOLUCION**: Product return
- **INTERCAMBIO**: Product exchange

### Session Management

Tracks delivery sessions:
- Route start/end times
- Driver assignments
- Financial totals
- Product quantities delivered

### Reporting

Provides comprehensive reports:
- Daily delivery summaries
- Sales by route/client
- Driver performance
- Product movement

For more details, see [Delivery Management](/features/delivery-management).
